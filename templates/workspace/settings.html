About Import from Jasmin

Joyce can pull groups, users, SMPP connectors, routes, and interceptors from your gateway. Filters are different: Jasmin does not expose them as their own list. They only exist on the active Jasmin session, attached to routes and interceptors.

When you import, Joyce will try to derive filters from those routes and interceptors. If that fails for a filter, it cannot be loaded into Joyce.

Also: HTTP connectors come from MO routes only (no HTTP PB). User passwords cannot be recovered from Jasmin (MD5 hashes).
